
KCET 2024 Physics - Laws of Motion

Question 1: A body of mass 5 kg is acted upon by a net force of 20 N. What is its acceleration?
Answer: a = F/m = 20/5 = 4 m/s²
Topic: Newton's Second Law
Year: 2024

Question 2: A ball is thrown vertically upward with velocity 19.6 m/s. How long does it take to reach maximum height?
Answer: t = v/g = 19.6/9.8 = 2 seconds
Topic: Projectile Motion
Year: 2024

Question 3: Two forces of 3N and 4N act at right angles. What is the resultant force?
Answer: R = √(3² + 4²) = √25 = 5N
Topic: Vector Addition
Year: 2025

Question 4: A car accelerates from rest to 72 km/h in 10 seconds. What is the acceleration?
Answer: 72 km/h = 20 m/s. a = (20-0)/10 = 2 m/s²
Topic: Kinematics
Year: 2025
